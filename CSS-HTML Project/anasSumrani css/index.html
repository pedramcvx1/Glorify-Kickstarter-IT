EL Espanyol

If you’re looking for reasons to visit Spain, look no further than its architecture. And it’s not just one famous building or even one particular style – there are incredible examples of multiple architectural styles across the country. Most famous are those by Antoni Gaudi, who was a key figure in the Catalan Modernist movement. His best-known buildings, namely the Sagrada Familia and Casa Batllo, are in Barcelona, though some of his works are found elsewhere in Spain.
